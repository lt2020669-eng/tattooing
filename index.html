Before & Aftercare

A few simple steps before and after your session can make healing smoother and help your tattoo settle beautifully.

Before You Book

  • Get a full night's sleep and eat well before your appointment.
  • Avoid alcohol the day before, and limit caffeine right before the session.
  • Do not tattoo on sunburned, irritated, or freshly injured skin.
  • Wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the tattoo area.
  • Please tell us in advance if you have allergies, skin conditions, or special healing concerns.

After Your Session

  • Keep the tattoo clean and follow the wrapping guidance from your artist.
  • Wash gently with lukewarm water and mild soap, then pat dry.
  • Apply a thin layer of recommended aftercare cream without over-moisturizing.
  • Avoid pools, hot baths, saunas, and intense sun exposure while healing.
  • Do not scratch or pick at flakes; let the skin heal naturally.

These are general guidelines for most appointments. If your design, placement, or skin condition needs extra care, we will walk you through it personally.

How It Works

From booking to arrival, here is exactly what happens next so you know when to fill in your form, pay the deposit, and prepare for your session.

1. Book a time

Open our Calendly page, choose the time that suits you best, and complete the appointment booking first.

2. Complete the pre-tattoo form

After booking, you will be guided to a short pre-tattoo form so we can understand your design idea, health notes, and communication needs.

3. Pay the deposit

Once the form is completed, you will receive the Stripe payment step. A 1100 JPY deposit is required to confirm your appointment.

4. Arrive for your session

On the day of your tattoo, we will review the placement and final details with you before starting the session.
